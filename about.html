About Boomerang Security Services

A trusted security partner with over 25 years of experience protecting what matters most.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Boomerang Security Services has grown from a small local security firm to one of the leading security providers in the nation. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional security services that protect people, property, and assets.

Over the years, we have expanded our services and expertise, but our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering. Today, we serve clients across various industries, from corporate offices and retail establishments to residential communities and special events.
