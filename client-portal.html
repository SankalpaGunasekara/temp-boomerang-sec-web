Portal Features

Our client portal provides a comprehensive suite of tools to help you manage your security services and stay informed.

Security Reports

Access detailed security reports, incident logs, and daily activity summaries. Stay informed about security events at your property or during your events.

Analytics Dashboard

View security trends, patterns, and statistics through our intuitive analytics dashboard. Make data-driven decisions to enhance your security measures.

Guard Management

View your assigned security team, their schedules, and qualifications. Request changes or additional personnel for special events or changing security needs.

Service Management

Manage your security services, request additional services, or modify existing ones. View your service history and upcoming scheduled services.

Access Control

Manage access credentials, view access logs, and control who can enter your facility. Add or remove authorized personnel as needed.

Billing & Invoices

Access your billing information, view and download invoices, and manage payment methods. Track your security service expenses and payment history in one convenient location.
