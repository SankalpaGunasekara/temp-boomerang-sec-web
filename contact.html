Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our security services.

How quickly can you respond to a security emergency?

Our average response time is under 15 minutes for most locations. For clients with on-site security personnel, response is immediate. We also maintain a 24/7 monitoring center for rapid dispatch of security teams.

Are your security guards armed or unarmed?

We offer both armed and unarmed security services depending on your specific needs and risk assessment. All our armed guards are properly licensed, certified, and undergo rigorous training.

How are your security personnel trained?

All our security personnel undergo comprehensive training that exceeds industry standards. This includes security protocols, emergency response, conflict resolution, first aid, and customer service. Armed guards receive additional specialized training.

Can you provide security for a one-time event?

Yes, we offer event security services for one-time events of all sizes. Our team will work with you to develop a comprehensive security plan tailored to your specific event needs.
